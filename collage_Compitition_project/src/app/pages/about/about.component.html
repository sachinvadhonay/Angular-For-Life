About TechNova

Empowering students to innovate, compete, and build real-world solutions

Who We Are

TechNova is a student-focused technology platform created to encourage innovation, creativity, and problem-solving among college students. We organize project-based competitions that help students transform ideas into real-world applications.

Our goal is to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry expectations by providing hands-on opportunities through competitions and collaborative projects.

About the Competition

The TechNova Project Competition is an open platform where students from different colleges can participate by submitting innovative projects in various domains such as Web Development, AI & Machine Learning, IoT, and Software Solutions.

  • Open to all undergraduate and postgraduate students
  • Team-based or individual participation
  • Real-world problem statements
  • Transparent evaluation process
